Isuzu Trooper

Explore the Legacy of the Isuzu Trooper

The Isuzu Trooper is a legendary mid-size SUV that blends rugged reliability with off-road versatility. Known for its durable build and long-lasting performance, the Trooper remains a symbol of adventure and capability.

  • Robust Powertrain: Featuring a 3.2L V6 engine or a turbo diesel variant, the Trooper delivers strong performance on and off the road.
  • 4WD Ready: With a part-time four-wheel-drive system and high ground clearance, it's built for rocky trails, mountain passes, and challenging terrain.
  • Spacious Interior: The Trooper offers a roomy cabin with seating for up to 7 passengers and foldable rear seats for additional cargo space.
  • Proven Reliability: A favorite among off-roaders and overlanders, the Trooper has earned a reputation for enduring reliability in harsh conditions.
  • Classic Design: With bold, boxy styling and a high stance, the Trooper looks as tough as it performs.
